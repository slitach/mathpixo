About Mathpixo

Making mathematical content accessible and digitizable

Mathpixo is a modern productivity tool built to solve a persistent bottleneck in STEM workflows: digitizing mathematics. We believe that equations, graphs, and control block diagrams should be as easy to type, search, and edit as plain text.

By combining advanced multimodal AI (using Google Gemini 2.5 Flash) with custom rendering libraries, Mathpixo allows researchers, students, and educators to instantly convert screenshots, photos, and full PDF documents of formulas into high-quality, compilable LaTeX code.

Core Technology

OCR Translation

Recognizes complex inline expressions, matrices, integrals, fractions, Greek letters, and alignment structures instantly.

PDF Document scanning

Supports processing complete PDF documents and pages, letting you digitize full textbook pages or research sheets.

TikZ & pgfplots Generation

Converts function plots, coordinate axes, and control system block diagrams into clean TikZ/ pgfplots drawing vectors.

Workspace Syncing

Save your work history securely to your account. Edit and preview equations inside your persistent cloud Snip space.

Our Vision

Writing LaTeX code manually is time-consuming. Our vision is to eliminate the friction in scientific and technical writing, helping authors and students focus on the content rather than the syntax. Whether you are copy-pasting equations into Overleaf or building course lecture notes, Mathpixo is your ultimate companion.
